Widgets
beta
Recreating one of Mac OS X's best features.
  • Overlay
  • Classic fullscreen overlay. Click the icon to activate or set up screen corners and the widgets fly to the desktop. Hold down shift to see them animate in slo-mo. Click anywhere on screen to make them fly away.
  • Weather
  • Check weather conditions worldwide, including current temperature, daily highs and lows, and a six-day forecast.
  • Calendar
  • This widget provides quick access to daily and monthly calendars. See the current date or click to view all days of the month.
  • Address Book
  • Find contact information quickly by browsing your Address Book or searching for specific entries.
  • Stocks
  • Keep track of stock performance for your favorite domestic and international funds, including live prices and trading activity.
  • Stickies
  • Add as many sticky note widgets as you need for reminders. Customize each note by flipping to change its color.
  • Unit Converter
  • Quickly convert between units for area, currency, length, speed, temperature, weight, and other measurements.
  • Calculator
  • Handle basic math operations quickly with this calculator widget. Includes addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and memory functions.
  • Clock
  • See the current time in any city worldwide. Display multiple clocks to track different time zones at once.
